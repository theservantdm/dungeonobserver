What is Dungeon Observer?

Dungeon Observer is a web-based, zero-player, rogue-like game.
Web-based? It runs in your browser (no download / install required).
Zero-player? It doesn't require any interaction from you.
Rogue-like? It's unforgiving, character death is permanent.
